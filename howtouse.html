📋 Step-by-Step Guide

1 Access the Image Generator

Navigate to the home page by clicking on "Home" in the navigation bar or go directly to index.html.

2 Enter Your Text Prompt

In the left panel, you'll find a text input field labeled "Enter a Text to Generate The Image". Click on this field and type your detailed description.

Example prompts: • "A sunset over mountains with golden clouds" • "A cute cat sitting in a flower garden" • "Futuristic city with flying cars at night" • "Abstract painting with vibrant colors and geometric shapes"
3 Generate Your Image

Click the "Generate Image" button to start the AI image creation process. The system will process your prompt and create a unique image.

💡 Pro Tip: Be specific in your descriptions for better results. Include details about colors, style, mood, and composition.
4 View Your Generated Image

Your generated image will appear in the right panel within the purple-bordered image box. The image will replace the default placeholder.

✍️ Writing Effective Prompts

The quality of your generated image largely depends on how well you describe what you want. Here are some tips for writing effective prompts:

Prompt Structure

[Subject] + [Action/Pose] + [Environment/Setting] + [Style/Mood] + [Additional Details] Example: "A majestic eagle soaring through cloudy skies above snow-capped mountains, photorealistic style, golden hour lighting"

Best Practices

  • Be Specific: Instead of "a dog", try "a golden retriever puppy playing in a green meadow"
  • Include Style: Mention artistic styles like "watercolor", "digital art", "photorealistic", or "cartoon style"
  • Describe Lighting: Add lighting conditions like "soft morning light", "dramatic shadows", or "neon lighting"
  • Add Colors: Specify color schemes like "warm autumn colors", "cool blue tones", or "vibrant rainbow colors"
  • Set the Mood: Include emotional descriptors like "peaceful", "mysterious", "energetic", or "serene"

🔧 Troubleshooting

Common Issues and Solutions

⚠️ Image Not Generating
  • Check your internet connection
  • Make sure your prompt is not empty
  • Try refreshing the page
  • Ensure JavaScript is enabled in your browser
⚠️ Download Not Working
  • Check if pop-ups are blocked in your browser
  • Ensure you have generated an image first
  • Try right-clicking the image and selecting "Save image as"
  • Clear your browser cache and try again
⚠️ Poor Image Quality
  • Try being more specific in your prompt
  • Add style descriptors like "high quality", "detailed"
  • Avoid contradictory instructions in your prompt
  • Consider creating an account for higher resolution options

💡 Tips and Tricks

Advanced Techniques

  • Combine Styles: Mix different art styles like "watercolor meets digital art" or "realistic photography with fantasy elements"
  • Use Negative Space: Describe what you don't want: "bright sunny day, no clouds, no shadows"
  • Reference Famous Artists: "In the style of Van Gogh" or "Picasso-inspired cubist portrait"
  • Specify Camera Settings: "Shot with 85mm lens, shallow depth of field, bokeh background"
  • Add Texture Details: "Rough wooden texture", "smooth marble surface", "soft fabric material"
